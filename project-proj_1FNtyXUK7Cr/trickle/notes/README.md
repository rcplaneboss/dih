# DIH Accessories&Gadgets - Store Management System

A comprehensive retail management system designed for DIH Accessories&Gadgets store operations with enhanced tracking and analytics.

## Features

### ✅ Multi-Brand Product Support
- Track products by type and brand (e.g., Power Banks: Oraimo, New Age, Remax)
- Separate inventory tracking for each brand variant
- Brand-specific pricing and cost management

### ✅ Inventory Management
- Add/edit products with brand categorization
- Real-time stock tracking with editable quantities
- Low stock alerts with customizable thresholds
- Product categorization (Power Banks, USB Cords, Chargers, etc.)

### ✅ Enhanced Sales & Transactions
- Quick sale recording with quantity selection
- Discount application and payment method tracking
- Customer notes for transaction records
- Automatic inventory deduction after sales
- **Serial numbered sales tracking with batch organization**
- **Date and time stamped transactions for precise tracking**
- **Daily sales entry with bulk processing and batch notes**
- **Multi-product sales processing with automatic inventory updates**
- **Profit calculation and tracking per transaction**

### ✅ Expenses Management
- **Comprehensive expense tracking with categories**
- **Date and time stamped expense records**
- **Multiple payment method support**
- **Real-time profit calculation (Sales - Expenses)**
- **Expense history with detailed notes**
- **Daily, monthly, and total expense analytics**

### ✅ Dashboard & Analytics
- Today's sales overview
- Low stock alerts with brand details
- Quick action buttons for common tasks
- Recent products display

### ✅ Mobile-First Design
- Bottom navigation for easy thumb access
- Touch-friendly interface optimized for phone use
- Clean, professional design with clear branding
- Responsive layout for various screen sizes

## Technical Implementation

### Data Storage
- Local storage for offline functionality
- Automatic data persistence
- Export/backup capabilities

### Product Categories Supported
- Power Banks, USB Cords, Wall Adapters, Chargers
- Batteries, Small Phones, Memory Cards, Flash Drives
- Headsets, Car Chargers, Smart Watches, Airpods
- Screen Protectors, Phone Pouches, and more

### Brand Management
Each product category includes common brands:
- Power Banks: Oraimo, New Age, Remax, Anker, Xiaomi
- USB Cords: Oraimo, New Age, Remax, Samsung, Apple
- And many more predefined brand options

## Usage

1. **Dashboard**: Overview of sales, inventory, and alerts
2. **Inventory**: Manage products, add new items, edit stock
3. **Daily Sales**: Record multiple sales at once with automatic inventory updates
4. **Sales History**: View transaction history and sales records
5. **Expenses**: Track and manage all business expenses with categories
6. **Categories**: Manage product categories and brands
7. **Reports**: Analytics and business insights

Perfect for small to medium retail stores specializing in phone accessories and gadgets.
